**Stage 5 (Energy Consumption):**
- **CRITICAL**: Exactly 24 hourly data points for EACH member (no exceptions!)
- **CRITICAL**: Exactly 24 hourly HVAC heating data points (no exceptions!)
- **CRITICAL**: Exactly 24 hourly HVAC cooling data points (no exceptions!)
- Hour values sequential (0-23)
- Consumption values ≥ 0 kWh
- **Consumption = 0 when member is outside the house is IDEAL but not critical** - small consumption while at work is acceptable
- Typical appliance consumption ranges (guidelines, not strict limits):
  * Sleeping: 0.01-0.05 kWh
  * Cooking: 0.1-0.8 kWh
  * TV/Entertainment: 0.05-0.4 kWh
  * HVAC: 0.3-4.0 kWh (varies widely)
- HVAC usage MUST be consistent with weather conditions:
  * Cooling MUST be non-zero during sustained high temperatures when occupants are home
  * Heating MUST be non-zero during sustained low temperatures when occupants are home
- HVAC consumption when no members are home MUST be significantly lower than when the house is occupied, unless justified by extreme weather conditions.
